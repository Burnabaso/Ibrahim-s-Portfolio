How I see Life...

If I can describe myself in one word, it will be "Passionate"! I believe that having passion for something leads to success in it. That is why I spend time knowing what I like to do, and what represents me as Ibrahim. When I find this passion, it becomes part of who I am.

Optimism in life means acknowledging life’s hardships and knowing that at the end of the day what counts is our efforts to resolve the issue despite the result. I try my best to keep a smile on my face and sprinkle of optimism in my life because without those two things, life would become a prison amidst the blessings I have in life.

From a Mechatronics Engineer to a Full-Stack Web Developer

As you will read in my education page, I acquire a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Mechatronics Engineering. During my major studies I decided to minor in computer science, this decision allowed me to take extra courses related to the field of programming. During these four years I enjoyed both fields. I enjoyed the understanding of robotic and control systems as well as the theory of software engineering, object-oriented programming, and web development. After completing the latter course, I found a new passion. I found a glimpse of who Ibrahim is. I found a career path that I am willing to take, embracing its difficulties and enjoying its privileges. After graduating I took the decision to make a career shift and study full stack web development. It was a hard decision but the passion I had and discipline I built up provided me with enough drive to go through the journey of self-learning “Full Stack Web Development”. Some might say why waste four years on a field that you will not pursue a career in. The way I perceive it is as follows: In those four years I learned how to think as an engineer, as a problem solver as a critical thinker and grew as an interactive person within my environment. I do not believe in mistakes; I only believe that life is series of decisions that we make. Those decisions lead to experiences that shape who we are and will shape our future selves. I am privileged to introduce myself as a self-taught full stack web developer in the making, and hope you” enjoy my portfolio that I have built as my first project” during my learning path.
